XEL REBIRTH: Reviving the Decentralized Computing Platform

A solo developer initiative to bring back XEL - the decentralized supercomputing network

About the Revival

XEL was a pioneering decentralized computing platform that allowed anyone to harness the power of distributed computing resources for complex computational tasks.

I'm reviving XEL because I believe in its original vision: making supercomputing accessible to everyone through blockchain technology, not just those with access to expensive hardware.

The future of XEL will focus on simplicity, accessibility, and practical applications - creating a network where computational power can be shared and utilized by anyone with an internet connection.
